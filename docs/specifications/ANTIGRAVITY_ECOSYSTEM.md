# The Antigravity Ecosystem

The Antigravity platform is not a single abstract idea. It is a decoupled, highly composable suite of research instruments designed to evaluate, validate, and track scientific discovery in the age of generative models.

## Product Hierarchy

### Product 1: AlignEval
**Status:** Working & Deployable
**Description:** Blind human-AI evaluation platform. Eradicates hindsight and ordering bias in expert surveys, allowing researchers to gather clean consensus data.

### Product 2: ConstraintBench
**Status:** Benchmark Constructed. Awaiting Independent Validation.
**Description:** Structural hallucination benchmark. An open-source corpus designed to measure how often frontier foundation models (GPT, Claude, Gemini) violate fundamental physical, mathematical, and historical constraints. 

### Product 3: AIDP (Artificial Intelligence Discovery Platform)
**Status:** Long-term Research Vision
**Description:** The overarching hindsight-resistant scientific discovery evaluation framework. AIDP acts as the primary "Research Program Under Evaluation," testing whether swarms of specialized agents can independently discover historical paradigm shifts without cheating.

---

## Strategy: Evidence First
By separating the immediate utility tools (AlignEval, ConstraintBench) from the long-term generative vision (AIDP), Antigravity ensures that it delivers immediate, measurable value to the scientific community regardless of whether the ultimate goal of AI-driven discovery succeeds or fails.
