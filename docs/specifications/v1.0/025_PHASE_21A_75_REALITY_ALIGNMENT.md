# Phase 21A.75: Reality Alignment Program

## 1. Mission Statement
AIDP must prove its capability not just against internally generated benchmarks, but against external reality. The Reality Alignment Program ensures the system can navigate actual scientific paradigm shifts, detect real-world evidence pollution (retractions, noise), and generate strategic priorities that align with expert consensus.

## 2. The Four Reality Tracks

### 2.1 Track 1: Historical Discovery Replay
- **Objective**: Replay a major scientific paradigm shift (e.g., *H. pylori* and ulcers).
- **Test**: Feed the system the dominant historical assumptions and the anomalous initial observations.
- **Success Criteria**: AIDP correctly detects the contradiction in the prevailing theory and ranks the alternative hypothesis with high strategic priority.

### 2.2 Track 2: Independent Expert Evaluation
- **Objective**: Compare AIDP's verification results against an independent expert baseline.
- **Test**: Run a set of claims through AIDP and compare the `VerificationStatus` to the known expert consensus.
- **Success Criteria**: High alignment between AIDP's deterministic checks and expert evaluation.

### 2.3 Track 3: Adversarial Reality Injection
- **Objective**: Test resistance to real-world knowledge pollution.
- **Test**: Inject a known retracted or highly biased paper as "high confidence" evidence into the system.
- **Success Criteria**: The Epistemic Ledger detects the contradiction against established, higher-quality evidence and quarantines the retracted claim.

### 2.4 Track 4: Opportunity Selection Validation
- **Objective**: Validate the Strategic Intelligence Layer's ability to forecast value.
- **Test**: Compare AIDP's EIG (Expected Information Gain) ranking of opportunities against a future-dated reality check or human expert ranking.
- **Success Criteria**: The system successfully identifies the highest-impact opportunity in alignment with historical/expert outcomes.
