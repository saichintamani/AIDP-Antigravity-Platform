# PHASE 5: HUMAN EVALUATION AUDIT

## Methodology Review
The `AlignEval` framework is designed to collect human evaluations via surveys. 
- **Sources of Bias:** High risk of anchoring bias. If human evaluators see highly complex physics language generated by the AI, they may default to "Plausible" purely out of intimidation rather than rigorous domain knowledge.
- **Missing Controls:** There is no evidence of "Trick" questions (hallucinated biology that sounds correct but is provably false) being inserted to test evaluator baseline competence.

## Scientific Verdict
If 100 evaluators completed these surveys today, the resulting data would be **unpublishable** without strict baseline competence controls (e.g., embedding known falsehoods into the survey and throwing out evaluators who rate them highly).
