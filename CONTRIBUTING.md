# Contributing to Antigravity

**Antigravity does not seek confirmation. Antigravity seeks reality.**

A successful review or contribution is not one that agrees with the authors. A successful review is one that increases confidence that the observed temporal leakage phenomenon is either real, misunderstood, partially true, or false.

## The Prime Directive

Every reproduction attempt must be preserved.
Every failed replication must be preserved.
Every hostile critique must be preserved.
Every alternative explanation must be preserved.

The objective of this repository is not to prove that temporal leakage exists because of a specific mechanism. The objective is to determine, with increasing confidence, what is actually happening when language models are asked to reason under epistemic constraints.

If the original hypothesis survives, that is a result.
If the original hypothesis fails, that is also a result.

Reality is the final reviewer.

## How to Contribute

We are currently in **Phase V: External Reality Acquisition**. We are no longer accepting pull requests for new cognitive architectures, more agents, or dashboard UI updates.

We are strictly looking for:
1. **Independent Reproductions**: Run the benchmark and submit your raw logs to `REPRODUCTION_LOG.md`.
2. **Hostile Reviews**: Falsify our methodology. Submit your criticisms to `data/ANTIGRAVITY_EVIDENCE_V1/THREATS_TO_VALIDITY.md`.
3. **Cross-Model Validation**: Run the benchmark on untested model families (Qwen, DeepSeek, Claude) and submit the metrics to `CROSS_MODEL_PROGRAM.md`.
4. **Alternative Explanations**: Provide mathematically or empirically sound alternative hypotheses for the observed behaviors.

If you are ready to test reality, please begin by reading `REPRODUCTION_PROTOCOL.md`.
