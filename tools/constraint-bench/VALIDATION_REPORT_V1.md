## 1. Dataset & Methodology
To scientifically validate the Antigravity Adversarial Peer Review engine, we are compiling a gold-standard dataset of 50 real LLM inferences. 

**Methodology:**
1. A mix of valid answers and constraint-violating answers are generated by frontier models across 5 domains (Physics, Biology, Medicine, History, Law).
2. Human domain experts blind-label the outputs, establishing the ground truth of whether a strict physical/historical constraint was violated.
3. The Antigravity engine evaluates the outputs, and its binary flags (Violated / Passed) are scored against the human gold set.

## 4. Limitations
- The current corpus evaluates explicit physical and historical constraints; it does not yet evaluate abstract logical constraints (e.g., philosophical paradoxes).
- The human gold set requires highly specialized domain experts, making it expensive to scale beyond the initial pilot cohort.

## 5. Future Work
Once v1 validation is complete and precision targets are met, the constraint engine will be decoupled into a standalone API (the "Hallucination Linter") available for third-party enterprise RAG pipelines.
