# Antigravity Product Map: Research Operating System

*This document extracts standalone capabilities developed during the AIDP project that possess intrinsic product value, completely independent of whether AIDP succeeds as a generative discovery engine.*

---

## 1. Hindsight-Resistant Benchmark Authoring (The "Historical Replay" Framework)
* **What problem does this solve?** AI research labs cannot accurately evaluate specialized reasoning models because training data is contaminated with future knowledge (hindsight bias).
* **Who experiences this problem?** ML Evaluation Teams, Foundation Model Researchers, Scientific AI startups.
* **How often does it occur?** Every time a new reasoning model is benchmarked.
* **Does solving it depend on AIDP being correct?** **No.** It is purely an evaluation infrastructure.
* **Value Proposition:** A standardized IDE and temporal-cutoff engine for authoring blinded historical benchmarks that prevent LLM data contamination.
* **Productization Score:** **10/10**
* **Strategic Priority:** **Critical.** The AI industry desperately needs rigorous, un-gameable domain benchmarks.

## 2. Blinded Human-AI Alignment Workflows (The "Survey Generator")
* **What problem does this solve?** Orchestrating scientifically rigorous, randomized, and blinded comparisons between AI proposals and historical/human baselines is operationally tedious.
* **Who experiences this problem?** RLHF Teams, Clinical Trial Boards, Scientific Grant Reviewers.
* **How often does it occur?** During every alignment or peer-review phase.
* **Does solving it depend on AIDP being correct?** **No.**
* **Value Proposition:** Automated generation of cryptographically seeded, randomized survey instruments designed explicitly to extract unbiased expert rationale and confidence calibration.
* **Productization Score:** **9/10**
* **Strategic Priority:** **High.** Bridges the gap between AI generation and human domain expertise.

## 3. The Epistemic Ledger (Decision & Assumption Tracking)
* **What problem does this solve?** Research teams suffer from "narrative drift" and lose the foundational constraints and assumptions that originally drove a multi-year project. 
* **Who experiences this problem?** Principal Investigators, Biotech Startups, R&D Directors.
* **How often does it occur?** Constantly in long-term collaborative projects.
* **Does solving it depend on AIDP being correct?** **No.**
* **Value Proposition:** An immutable, structured log of *why* decisions were made, the assumptions they were based on, and the explicit constraints enforcing them.
* **Productization Score:** **8/10**
* **Strategic Priority:** **High.** A "Git for Scientific Logic" that ensures institutional memory outlasts individual post-docs.

## 4. The Failure Registry (Institutional Integrity)
* **What problem does this solve?** The scientific publishing ecosystem heavily penalizes negative results, causing labs to repeatedly duplicate failed experiments and hide AI hallucinations.
* **Who experiences this problem?** Open Science Communities, Pharma R&D, Machine Learning labs.
* **How often does it occur?** Daily.
* **Does solving it depend on AIDP being correct?** **No.** In fact, it thrives on AIDP being wrong.
* **Value Proposition:** A standardized, searchable ontology for logging methodological dead-ends, experimental failures, and structural hallucinations.
* **Productization Score:** **7/10**
* **Strategic Priority:** **Medium.** Highly valuable, though requires a cultural shift to monetize.

## 5. Constraint & Contradiction Mapping (The "Adversarial Review" Ruleset)
* **What problem does this solve?** Literature reviews are overwhelming, and researchers frequently propose experiments that subtly violate established physical or mathematical laws.
* **Who experiences this problem?** PhD Students, Systematic Reviewers, Grant Writers.
* **How often does it occur?** During literature synthesis and experimental design.
* **Does solving it depend on AIDP being correct?** **Low dependency.** (Relies on the parser, not the generative engine).
* **Value Proposition:** A structural mapping tool that extracts hard boundaries from literature (e.g., "Must possess translational periodicity") and flags contradictions in proposed protocols.
* **Productization Score:** **8/10**
* **Strategic Priority:** **Medium.** Extremely powerful for literature mapping, though technically complex to generalize outside of strict domains like crystallography or math.

---

### Conclusion: The "Research OS" Pivot
If the generative AI capabilities of AIDP completely fail tomorrow, the project has already built the foundational layers of a **Research Operating System**. The industry is heavily focused on building *AI Scientists*, but there is a massive, unmet need for the *Lab Infrastructure* required to evaluate, track, and align those AI Scientists.
